We love to hear from people who are interested in helping out the project.

Thank you for submitting your information to PRISM!

PRISM is a platform for critical information that governments, NGOs, and WFP need to respond to climate disasters in an effective manner. It combines remote sensing data, survey data, and on-the-ground data from mobile phones into a single information risk analytics platform.

We are looking for support to scale up the PRISM platform to more countries and adding additional functionality, including more data sources, Machine Learning, and better visualizations. To help us reach the next level, we need world-class software engineers, data scientists, and systems architects.

Please reach out to us if you are interested in hearing more about what we're building, sharing your network, mentoring, or volunteering your own skills. Together, we can save lives and protect livelihoods from climate disasters.
